# Privacy Policy — GhostCart

GhostCart respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data.

## 1. Information We Collect
GhostCart does not require account creation.

The app may store data locally on your device, including:
- urges you log
- decisions (buy / skip)
- timestamps related to your activity

This data is stored only on your device.

## 2. Advertising
GhostCart may display ads using third-party services (e.g., Google AdMob).

These services may collect:
- device information
- advertising identifiers

This data is used to serve relevant ads.

## 3. Purchases
GhostCart uses third-party services (RevenueCat and Google Play Billing) to handle subscriptions.

These services may collect:
- purchase history
- subscription status

GhostCart does not store or process payment information directly.

## 4. Data Storage
All user-generated data is stored locally on your device.

We do not collect or store personal data on external servers.

## 5. Your Control
You can:
- delete your data by uninstalling the app
- reset your data from within the app
